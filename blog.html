Belkin WEMO remote switch / power usage monitor

BUY IT ON AMAZON

The same one as mine is currently unavailable on AMAZON so I hooked up links to similar one.

You can control this remote switch through the internet, even from the other country using application on your smartphone.

You can plug anything you want to this switch. I am using it to be able to remotely restart my Ethereum miner, but you can use it for lights, and other devices to power up or restart them remotely.

You can also check power usage and cost using app. From my experience this device showing accurate power usage, however power cost monitor is not very accurate.

You can also put this switch on schedule, that's very useful when you are going to vacation. You can plug your lights to it and set them up to switch on every evening.
