Watch out for easy money websites


Easy money websites

There are a lot of easy money websites all over the internet. Most of them are a complete waste of time in my opinion.
The idea of making money from the comfort of your home is great, but you have to really watch out when you are looking for the solution to achieve it. There are websites where you can get paid for taking a survey, register to some gambling, shopping, TV series sites through the provided affiliate link etc.

Don't get me wrong because I love the affiliate marketing and thanks to Amazon Affiliate Program I can make some money on my blog.
If you like my content and you are using amazon.co.uk you can support my blog by going to Amazon from any link on the side of my blog before you buy.
Thanks if you do! :)

Going back to the topic of today's post, to make money on websites like 20cogs.co.uk, mysurvey.com and similar you have to register through provided link to some mostly gambling sites (some of them are SCAM sites) and top up your account there, buy subscription, fill long time-wasting surveys etc. and after you type in your credit card details, address, telephone number to 10, 20, 30 websites, waste the time on surveys you may still have the problem with payout.

I am not saying that all of this kind of websites are bad, you will find some genuine and safe websites for sure but read opinions especially the bad opinions about the website you are going to use for making money from home.
